Black autistic girls can often express their emotions in ways that may differ from what’s commonly expected. Our ways of showing joy, distress, or other feelings can be subtle and deeply personal, which might sometimes lead to misunderstandings. It's important to recognize and appreciate these unique expressions. How do you support and understand the emotional expressions of Black girls in your life? Let’s foster a deeper understanding and celebrate the diverse ways emotions are communicated.
